About

Juxtapoetry displays random images and texts next to each other, allowing the viewer's brain to construct a connecting narrative.

Tap the i icons in the lower corners to see information about a text or image's source. Tap any other part of the page to see the next random content.

By default, images are fetched from the Cleveland Museum of Art and Metropolitan Museum of Art's APIs, and text is fetched from the Poetry DB API.

Check out the site's source on GitHub.
